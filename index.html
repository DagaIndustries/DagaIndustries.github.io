ABOUT THE COMPANY

Daga Industries was established in the year 1973. Since then it has set trail blazing standards of technical excellence, scaling new heights in its determinations to deliver the best for 5 decades.
Daga Industries has supplied hi-tech equipments & spares to major core sectors such as Coal, Fertilizers & Chemicals, Paper, Aluminium, Sugar, Power, Cement, Glass etc. in India.
Daga Industries manufacture complete equipments i.e. Belt Conv. Drive Heads, Haulages, Screw & Rotary Feeders, Tipplers etc. & assure to deliver best performance with innovations.
